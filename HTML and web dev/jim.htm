The First Step

After a solid year of consideration, and around 6 months of asking, I finally had the support and funds to join the gym. On the 17th of January, I take a parent and sign my soul away to the world of fitness.

A False Start

I signed up, but none of the gym staff were around to book an induction for me. I understood that I was to hae an induction that very Thursday, but when I turned up there were no slots available. I was booked for the following Thursday instead.

The Induction

On Thursday 26th January, I had my induction. Since that point, I was allowed to enter and use the gym whenever my membership permitted. I was also qualified to use all machines in the general area. I began a habit of attempting to go 2 times a week minimum, though more recently this goal has progressed to 3 times per week.

Progression

On Thursday the 2nd of march I took my freeweights induction, where I learned (and promptly forgot) the correct form for squats, deadlifts, and horizontal bench.
I haven't set a PR for squat yet, but for deadlift the most I have lifted is 50kg, and for bench I have a one-rep max of 30kg. Obviously this all needs improving by quite a stretch.
