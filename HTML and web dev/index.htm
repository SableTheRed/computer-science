Why YOU should play Dungeons and Dragons

What is it?

Dungeons and Dragons is a table top roleplaying game that encouragers players to solve problems and interact in a fantasy world. The Dungeon Master (DM) acts as storyteller and worldbuilder, creating interesting problems for the players to work through. The remaining players will form a party of unique characters of their own creation, before embarking on an epic quest of combined imagination.

Reasons to play

  1. Develops communication skills
  2. Improves problem-solving and lateral thinking
  3. Facilitates teambuilding
  4. It's great fun!
